This house is built on the principle of creating new knowledge; inventing.

We believe invention comes in many forms, but regardless of what you do it must be shared. Sharing your work ‚Äî both privately and publicly ‚Äî is the ultimate defense against delusion and a compass towards truth. Sharing knowledge exposes you to new and potentially better explanations, so to avoid sharing is to fear the truth.

Knowledge that is never shared is a tragedy, but it must also be done with care. Over-simplification and over-complication are almost as bad as not sharing.

Therefore the objective is to maximize that quantity of new knowledge created, subject to the constraint that it is shared with the world in its most honest representation.

Living here is a commitment to sharing the knowledge you create. That means:

  • Publishing papers
  • Open-sourcing hardware and/or software
  • Gathering and releasing valuable data

If these values resonate with you, apply to join us.
